Adsum IoT Coder is an AI coding agent for VS Code that owns your entire embedded inner loop on Espressif ESP and Nordic nRF platforms: scaffold, build, flash, test, observe, and fix. It automates the routine firmware work you would rather skip, and cracks the runtime bugs that general-purpose agents miss — because it reads your board, not just your code.

What sets it apart is real human expertise baked into the agent, not just a larger model. Adsum is augmented with curated firmware knowledge authored by engineers who have shipped production firmware, loaded on demand, and validated by an [open benchmark](/benchmark) on real hardware. Human-curated, not AI-generated.

## Shipping today

Adsum IoT Coder supports the most common embedded targets in production use today, with more on the way.

- **Nordic nRF52 / nRF53 / nRF54** on nRF Connect SDK (Zephyr)
- **Espressif ESP32, ESP32-S3, ESP32-C6** on ESP-IDF
- **BLE and Wi-Fi**
- Open source under Apache 2.0

Thread, Matter, LTE-M, and additional chips are on the [roadmap](/platforms-and-roadmap).
